Request data

Tell us about data you want added to the Data Workspace catalogue.

How long it takes

This is the first stage of the process and should take you about 5 minutes to complete. We will respond to this initial request within 5 working days. We may want to speak with you to confirm that we can support your request, and to discuss scope and timelines. The second stage will be to complete a 'data onboarding' form, which asks for more technical information.

If you feel your request is urgent and supports a time critical departmental priority, contact us via the support and feedback form.

Before you start

You will be asked to provide details on what the data is used for, where it is currently held and the names of the information asset owner or manager. You should have this information available before you start.
